How it works

Speak. Reflect. Understand.

🎙

Think out loud

Tap once and start talking. untangle transcribes your thoughts automatically — no typing, no blank page. Voice Activity Detection means you just speak, and it handles the rest.

✦

Daily summaries

Each day's notes are distilled into a structured summary — your thoughts, mood, ideas, decisions, and what you ate — with a unique piece of art generated just for that day.

◈

Insights that build a portrait of you

Over time, untangle builds a psychological picture from your words — your Big Five traits, Enneagram type, thinking patterns, blind spots, values, and the story arc of your life. Not a snapshot. A portrait.
