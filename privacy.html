Privacy Policy

Your journal stays yours.

Last updated: April 18, 2026  ·  App version 1.3.0

Short version: untangle is a personal journal. Your voice recordings, transcripts, photos, and notes live on your device. We never sell your data. Audio and text are sent to AI providers solely to generate your summaries and insights — nothing is stored on their servers beyond the duration of each request.

1. Who we are

untangle is an independently developed app by Priya Singh. If you have any questions about this policy, email us at privacy@liveuntangle.com.

2. What data untangle collects

Voice recordings
When you use the microphone, untangle records audio and stores it temporarily on your device. The audio is sent to OpenAI Whisper (via our secure server) to produce a text transcript. The audio file is deleted from our server immediately after transcription. The resulting transcript is stored on your device only.

Journal entries and transcripts
All journal entries, transcripts, summaries, and insights are stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage. We do not sync your journal to any cloud database. We do not have access to your journal content.

Photos
Photos you attach to journal entries are stored locally on your device. If a photo is included in a summary request, it is sent to Anthropic Claude (via our secure server) solely to generate your summary, and is not retained by us or Anthropic beyond that request.

Account & email address
If you sign in, your email address is stored by Supabase Authentication solely to identify your account session. We do not use it for marketing.

Wellbeing & mood data
If you use wellbeing check-ins, your mood and distress scores are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Expense data
If you log expenses, amounts, categories, and descriptions are stored locally on your device only.

ElevenLabs voice synthesis
If you use the Call feature, the text of your summary is sent to ElevenLabs (via our secure server) to generate a spoken audio response. The text is not retained by ElevenLabs beyond the duration of the request.

Wisdom Shorts
The Wisdom Shorts feed is loaded from our Supabase database. This request contains no personal information — only a request for the public content list.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or contacts
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell your data
  • We do not build profiles or share data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not have access to your journal entries or recordings
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID)

3. Third-party services we use

To provide AI-powered features, your content is processed by the following services. Each request is proxied through our server — your device never contacts these APIs directly, and API keys are never embedded in the app.

  • OpenAI (Whisper) — audio transcription.
    OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) — daily summaries and journal insights.
    Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech for the Call feature.
    ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — hosts the Wisdom Shorts database and our AI proxy functions.
    Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscription purchases. Receives your subscription status and purchase events.
    RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — product analytics. Receives anonymised event names (e.g. "recording_started", "summary_viewed") and your user ID. No journal content is ever included.
    PostHog Privacy Policy

4. Permissions

  • Microphone — to record your voice for journal entries.
  • Camera & Photo Library — to attach photos to journal entries.
  • Notifications — to alert you when your daily summary is ready.

5. Data retention and deletion

All your journal data (entries, transcripts, summaries, photos) is stored on your device. You can delete individual entries from within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

AI requests are not retained by us or our AI providers beyond the time required to generate a response (typically a few seconds).

6. Children's privacy

untangle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you within the app.
